## **Introduction**

Across **EdTech, Consumer Apps, FinTech, HealthTech, and SaaS**, user communication is the single most powerful, yet most poorly engineered, growth lever.

Today’s notification systems are:

* Rule-based
* Segment-blind
* Timing-agnostic
* Completely unaware of **what actually works for whom, when, and why**

By **2030**, intelligent communication orchestration will be the competitive moat — knowing:

* **WHO** to talk to
* **WHAT** to say
* **WHEN** to say it
* **WHY** it works
* **HOW** to improve continuously

Your challenge is to build that system:

> a **self-learning notification orchestrator** that can extend to any **B2C or B2B** application.

**EdTech (SpeakX)** is the reference domain, but your architecture must be **domain-agnostic** — swap the Knowledge Bank and user data, keep the same orchestrator core.

## **About SpeakX**

SpeakX is an **AI-powered English speaking and communication learning app** that helps Hindi and regional-language speakers in India transform from:

> “I understand English but can’t speak”
> to
> **confident English speakers**

This is achieved through:

* Daily guided speaking practice
* AI tutor conversations
* Gamification (coins, streaks, leaderboards)
* Progress reports
* Role-based practice scenarios

Target audience:

* Pan-India (Tier 1, 2, 3)
* Primary: Tier 2 & 3
* Age group: **20–45**
* Working professionals, college students, ambitious homemakers

Goal: **daily engagement and habit formation**

## **Key Metrics**

* **Trial → Monthly Conversion**
* **Monthly Retention**

  * Users who complete an exercise within the month after converting to paid
* **W1 Retention**

  * Users who complete an exercise in the last week after trial → paid conversion

Metrics vary significantly across **user personas**, presenting opportunities for improvement through **intelligent, personalized communication**.

### **2. Template Generator**

For **each**:

* Segment × Lifecycle × Goal × Theme

Create:

* **Exactly 5 message templates**
* Bilingual (Hindi + English)
* Include:

  * Content
  * Tone
  * Hook
  * Feature reference

### **3. Timing Optimizer**

Define **6 standard time windows**:

* early_morning: 06:00–08:59
* mid_morning: 09:00–11:59
* afternoon: 12:00–14:59
* late_afternoon: 15:00–17:59
* evening: 18:00–20:59
* night: 21:00–23:59

Learn optimal windows per segment.

---

### **4. Frequency Optimizer**

Determine daily notifications based on activeness:

| Activeness Score | Notifs/day | Churn Risk | Strategy       |
| ---------------- | ---------- | ---------- | -------------- |
| > 0.7            | 7–9        | Low        | Max engagement |
| 0.4–0.7          | 5–6        | Medium     | Balanced       |
| < 0.4            | 3–4        | High       | Conservative   |

**Guardrail Override:**
If `uninstall_rate > 2%` → reduce frequency by **2/day** regardless of activeness.
